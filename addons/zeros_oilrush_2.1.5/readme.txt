Zeros OilRush

Extract the ZeroOilRush folder do your addons located at 
"\Steam\steamapps\common\GarrysMod\garrysmod\addons"

The main configuration can be done in the zrush_config_main.lua which is located at 
"addon\zeros_oilrush\lua\"

Edit what you need in the config, after save and restart your server.
